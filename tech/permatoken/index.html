Welcome

The Positive Psychology Text Analyser allows you to easily compare any text to the World Wellbeing Project's PERMA lexica in English 🇬🇧 / 🇺🇸 or en Espa√±ol 🇪🇸, as well as many of their other lexica. Simply copy any text (transcripts, essays, etc.) into the textbox below, choose any additional options, and press Start!

It can also output your text as a tokenised CSV file. The tokeniser may have trouble with special characters and phrases such as HTML elements, web addresses and phone numbers. It is recommended that these are removed before tokenising if you wish to use that feature.
